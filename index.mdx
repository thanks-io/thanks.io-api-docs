thanks.io provides everything you need for direct mail success: AI Realistic Handwritten postcards, letters & notecards, QR tracking, delivery notifcations, automation, and texting all at super competitive pricing.

The thanks.io API enables developers to integrate direct mail capabilities into their applications. With our API you can send any mailpiece you can imagine or add a recipient to an existing thanks.io automation.  

## Easy Implementation Path

The most straightforward way to integrate with thanks.io is to add recipients to your customers thanks.io Mailing List. Then your client can set up automations within the thanks.io platform. All creatives, billing, and scheduling is handled by us.
